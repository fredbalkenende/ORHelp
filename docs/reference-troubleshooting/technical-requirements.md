# Technical requirements

Before you install OfficeReports, make sure your system meets the requirements below.

## Requirements

To run OfficeReports, you need:

- Windows 11 or 10
- Microsoft Office 365, 2021, 2019 2016 or 2013
- A 32-bit or 64-bit version of Office
- One of these Office editions:
  - Home
  - Professional
  - Professional Plus
  - Office 365 Business (desktop version)
  - Office 365 Business Premium (desktop version)

You do not need Administrator rights to install OfficeReports.
