# Uninstall OfficeReports

Follow the steps below to uninstall OfficeReports. Close Excel and PowerPoint before you start.

## Uninstall on Windows 10 and 11

1. Open **Settings**.
2. Click **Apps** and then **Installed Apps".
3. Find **OfficeReports** in the list.
4. Select **OfficeReports** and click on the three dots.
5. Click **Uninstall**.

You can also search for **OfficeReports** from the Windows taskbar and click **Uninstall**.
