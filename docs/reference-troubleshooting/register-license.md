# Handle the OfficeReports subscription

Use the OfficeReports App to register a license key, move a license to another machine, or cancel a subscription.

## Register a license key

1. Click **Register** in the OfficeReports App.
2. Enter your name.
3. Enter the license key you received by email after purchasing OfficeReports licenses.

![Register a License Key](/Resources/Images/RegisterLicense_809x896.png)

## Move the license to another machine

If you need to move your license to another machine, first deactivate the license on the old computer.

1. On the old computer, click **Deactivate license on this machine**.
2. Complete the deactivation in the window that opens.

![](/Resources/Images/OfficeReports Analytics/Move the License to another.png)

## Cancel the subscription

If you bought the OfficeReports subscription in the online shop, you can cancel it in one of these ways:

1. Click the **Subscription Cancelation** link in the email you received when you bought the subscription.
2. Send an email to [helpdesk@officereports.com](mailto:helpdesk@officereports.com).
