# OfficeReports Product Comparison

OfficeReports has three products for market research teams, each designed for a different part of the workflow.

## The simplest way to understand the difference

* **Presento** is for teams that already have results in Excel and want to automate PowerPoint reporting.
* **Calculo** is for teams that need to analyze survey data and create crosstabs in Excel.
* **Intelligo** is for teams that want to go from survey data to PowerPoint in one workflow.

## Start with your real need

Choose **Presento** if your analysis already exists and your main goal is to update PowerPoint deliverables automatically.

Choose **Calculo** if your main goal is to turn raw survey data into tables, banners, and statistical results in Excel.

Choose **Intelligo** if you need both: survey analysis and automated PowerPoint reporting.

## Which product should I choose?

### Choose Presento when:
* your data or results are already available in Excel
* you need to update PowerPoint charts, tables, and text automatically
* your main pain point is manual report production
* you do not need to create crosstabs from raw survey data in this tool

### Choose Calculo when:
* you need to analyze survey data in Excel
* you need crosstabs, banners, means, nets, and significance tests
* your workflow ends with tables and analysis output in Excel
* you do not need automated PowerPoint population

### Choose Intelligo when:
* you need to analyze survey data and automate PowerPoint reporting
* you want one workflow from data to presentation
* you create recurring tracker, wave, or cross-market reports
* you want to reduce both analysis work and reporting work

## Product Details

## Presento

**Main purpose:** Automate PowerPoint reporting from Excel-based results

Presento is designed for report automation. It links Excel content to PowerPoint and updates shapes, tables, charts, and text when the source data changes.

Presento is the right choice when the analysis has already been done and the main challenge is producing or updating PowerPoint deliverables efficiently.

### Presento is typically used for:
* recurring PowerPoint reports
* tracker presentations
* client reporting in PowerPoint
* updating existing charts and tables from Excel
* reducing manual slide production

### Presento is not designed for:
* creating crosstabs from raw survey data
* significance testing
* survey data processing and analysis

## Calculo

**Main purpose:** Analyze survey data and create crosstabs in Excel

Calculo is designed for market researchers who work with survey data in Excel. It supports cross tabulation, banner analysis, percentages, counts, means, nets, and significance testing.

Calculo is the right choice when your main job is analysis and table production, and PowerPoint automation is not required.

### Calculo is typically used for:
* crosstabs
* banner tables
* significance testing
* survey data analysis in Excel
* turning raw data into reporting tables

### Calculo is not designed for:
* automated PowerPoint slide population
* end-to-end presentation generation
* report automation in PowerPoint

## Intelligo

**Main purpose:** Combine survey analysis and PowerPoint reporting automation

Intelligo includes the survey analysis capabilities of Calculo and adds PowerPoint reporting automation. It is designed for teams that want to move from survey data to presentation output in one workflow.

Intelligo is the right choice when both parts matter: creating the results and delivering them in PowerPoint.

### Intelligo is typically used for:
* survey data analysis
* crosstabs and significance testing
* recurring tracker and wave reporting
* automated PowerPoint reporting
* end-to-end reporting workflows

### Intelligo includes:
* the core analysis capabilities used in Calculo
* PowerPoint integration
* automation support for reporting workflows

## Common Questions

### What is the difference between Calculo and Intelligo?

Calculo focuses on survey data analysis in Excel. Intelligo includes the same analysis functionality and also supports PowerPoint reporting automation.

### What is the difference between Presento and Intelligo?

Presento focuses on PowerPoint automation using Excel as the source. Intelligo also supports survey data analysis and cross tabulation.

### Can I use Presento for survey data analysis?

Presento is mainly for report automation in PowerPoint. For survey data analysis and cross tabulation, Calculo or Intelligo is the better choice.

### Can I use Calculo to automate PowerPoint slides?

Calculo is focused on analysis in Excel. If you need PowerPoint automation as well, Intelligo is the better fit.

### Which product is the most complete?

Intelligo is the most complete product because it combines survey analysis and PowerPoint reporting automation.
